Chao Feng (冯超, in Chinese)

I earned my PhD from the Xi'an Jiaotong University, China. I am co-advised by the Prof. Jin Liang and Prof. Leila De Floriani.

My research focuses on computer graphics with special interests in point cloud processing, polygon mesh processing, 3D human body scanning, as well as 3D optial reconstruction. My research profiles can be found at both Google Scholar.

Email: chayfung@hotmail.com

About

I was a postdoctoral fellow working with Prof. Peter Wonka at the Visual Computing Center, King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST), from 2014 to 2017. Before that, I recevied my B.S. in Computational Mathematics from Wuhan University in 2009. I got my Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics from Zhejiang University in 2014, under the supervision of Prof. Ligang Liu.

My research interests are in the area of computer graphics and computer vision.
