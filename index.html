Chao Feng (冯超, in Chinese)

I earned my PhD from the Xi'an Jiaotong University, China. I am co-advised by the Prof. Jin Liang and Prof. Leila De Floriani.

My research interests include Computer Graphics, Geometric Modeling, especially in 3D point cloud processing, polygon mesh processing, 3D automatic scanning, 3D human body scanning, as well as 3D optical reconstruction. My research can be found at Github.

Email: chayfung@hotmail.com
